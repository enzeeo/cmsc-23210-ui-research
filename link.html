Study Debrief

Thank you for participating in this study. As you may now be aware, the purpose of our study was not to evaluate the website's UI as we previously led you to believe, but rather to evaluate your engagement with online Terms and Condition agreements (T&C).

To accurately measure your natural behavior with T&C, we decided to withhold this information at the beginning of the study as your awareness may have changed how you interacted with the T&C. This potential change of behavior may bias our results, affecting the validity of our experiment. For these reasons, we felt that incomplete disclosure was necessary.

During the study, the T&C you encountered was the primary focus of the research. The website UI you were told you would evaluate was not part of the study.

You will now complete a survey to test your comprehension and understanding of the T&C. However, we would like to note that no sensitive personal information will be collected throughout this entire study, your participation does not involve any known risks beyond what may be expected from engaging with a T&C, and that your responses will remain confidential as our analysis will be completed using aggregated data only.

If you wish to withdraw your data now that you are fully informed please indicate this to a researcher or contact us using the information provided below. You will not receive any penalties for doing so.

If you have any questions about this study, please feel free to ask now or contact us:

ennako@uchicago.edu, bingning680@uchicago.edu, mchenain@uchicago.edu, mkim27@uchicago.edu, veronicasokoloff@uchicago.edu

If you have any questions regarding your rights as a participants, please contact the IRB:

https://sbsirb.uchicago.edu/aurairb/participants/

Please ensure you complete the survey before leaving. Thank you for your time and participation.
